Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our expense tracking app.

Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account, add expenses, and use Spendly. This may include transaction details, categories, dates, amounts, descriptions, and basic profile information.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide and improve the Spendly service. We use your information to display your expense history, generate spending summaries, and personalize your experience within the app.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

We store your data securely as part of the Spendly service. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Please do not share sensitive credentials or personal data in unsupported ways.

Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third party services to host content and support app functionality. These services may have their own privacy practices, and we recommend reviewing their policies if they collect data on your behalf.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the feedback or support option within the app.
